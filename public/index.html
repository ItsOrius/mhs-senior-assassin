RULES

  • Every participating player will be assigned a "target" in their emails.
  • Players will attempt to squirt a water gun at their target to eliminate them.
  • Players are also allowed to shoot their assassin, but they should be sure they're correct, because...
  • If a player squirts a water gun at somebody who isn't their target or assassin, they must either resign or work to create a compromise with the other person.
  • Once a player eliminates somebody else, they must report it WITH VIDEO EVIDENCE at https://marinercentral.net.
  • Confirmed eliminations will be posted on Instagram (@mhs.24.seniorassassin) with names and photos (where applicable).
  • MHS GROUNDS ARE A SAFE ZONE! We shouldn't do anything that a faculty member will get upset about...

The last person/people standing wins!
The winner(s), as well as the person with the most eliminations, will receive a small prize!
